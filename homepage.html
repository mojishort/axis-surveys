Tools & Tech We Use

High-resolution Drones

We use advanced drones equipped with 4K cameras and LiDAR to capture detailed aerial imagery. This allows us to map large areas quickly, monitor construction progress, and perform volumetric analysis with unmatched accuracy.

GNSS Receivers

Our Trimble and Leica GNSS receivers provide centimeter-level positioning accuracy. They are essential for boundary surveys, geodetic control, and any project requiring precise location data.

Laser Levels & Total Stations

These instruments allow us to perform highly accurate leveling, alignment, and angular measurements, making them indispensable for construction layout and topographic mapping.

GIS & CAD Software

We use GIS for spatial analysis and CAD for detailed engineering drawings. This combination ensures our survey data is transformed into actionable maps, site plans, and designs.
